COMPUTERSCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

About the Department

The department was established in the year 1987 with a UG programme in CSE. Since its inception, the department has progressed with a vision and a strong commitment to developing competent programmers with uncompromising standard of excellence in the training, both class room sessions and hands on sessions, given to students. The department has well equipped computing laboratories and a rich repository of software covering a wide spectrum of applications. It now offers PG and PhD programmes also.
