MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

About the Department

Department of Mechanical Engineering is one of the oldest departments of the college; our environment combines technical expertise with creativity and imagination. We offer modern, state-of the –art workshops, laboratories and computer facilities. The department of Mechanical Engineering has been contributing its humble share of the Mechanical Engineering graduates for national needs. Several of the graduates who have passed out of this department are occupying responsible positions in various Engineering Industries like (TCS, WIPRO, MAHINDRA, Bosch, and holding responsible positions in the premier educational institutes in and outside India. The Department of Mechanical Engineering has been recognized as a research centre by Anna University from April 2003.The department is actively engaged in research, our thrust areas are Welding, Metal cutting, Process optimization, Foundry, Composite Materials, Machine Scheduling, Energy Management.. The faculty members have published more than 150 technical papers in national and international seminars and journals of repute. At present there are 34 highly qualified, experienced and dedicated faculty members, many of them in middle level (Assistant Professors and Senior Lecturers). The faculty includes 7 Ph. D’s.
